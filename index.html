About

I am currently a second-year Ph.D. candidate at the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), advised by Prof. Linli Xu. My research interests include audio-visual multimodal learning, generative models, and vector quantization. Recently, I have been focusing on Native MLLMs, specifically exploring the unified processing of text, vision, and speech using a single encoder-decoder framework.
